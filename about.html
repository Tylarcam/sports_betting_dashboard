Scoring Framework

Score 2: Full Adoption

Both online/mobile and retail sports betting legally operational

Score 1: Partial Adoption

Only one channel (online/mobile OR retail) legally operational

Score 0: No Adoption

No legal sports betting or pending implementation

Additional Metrics

  • • Implementation speed (days from legalization to launch)
  • • Legalization method (legislation, referendum, tribal compact)
  • • Tax rate structure and revenue sharing models
  • • Market maturity and competitive landscape
